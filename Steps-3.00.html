Data Overview 3
Data Sources

The data for this study was collected and entered into the City-Parish Payroll System after the hiring phase, by the Baton Rouge Human Resources department. This data represents all employees who were employed by the department prior to July 2024. This division oversees administrative duties and acts as an advisor and liaison to the Personnel Board, Metropolitan Council, and other agencies.


System Architecture

The project combines Microsoft Fabric with Power BI for enhanced data visualization and reporting. Predictive models are developed using Python and Spark to enable scalable data processing.



Data Features

The dataset contained several key features essential for analyzing employee attrition in human resources.
  • Independent Variables: Years of Service, Scheduled Hours, Annual Salary
  • Dependent Variable: Employment Status
  • Calculated Metrics: Average Years of Service, Attrition Rate
